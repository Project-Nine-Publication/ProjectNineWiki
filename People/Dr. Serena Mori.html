Naming and Etymology

  • Love Zero concept outline - 森塔依博士. ChatGPT: Dr. Sylas Moriarty
  • Sylas: Inspired by 森 (forest), evoking growth, nature, and life—aligning with bioengineering themes.
  • Moriarty: Derived from "森" (forest) and "塔依" phonetically. It adds a scholarly and enigmatic air to the character.

Alternatively, if you prefer a simpler option: Dr. Serena Mori:

  • Serena: Echoes nature's tranquility, fitting for someone deeply connected to bioengineering.
  • Mori: Directly inspired by 森 (forest), simple yet elegant.
