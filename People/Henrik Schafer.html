A modern-day preacher who works in high-rise office as manager during days and is secretly a missionary. His great great ancestor (many thousand) years ago was first-named "Henry".

Naming and Etymology

  • I don't want him to sound too English-origin. Ideally German origin. Henrik: A German variation of "Henry," maintaining a connection to his ancestor. Schäfer: Meaning "shepherd" in German, subtly nodding to his role as a missionary or spiritual guide. Alternative last name: Kastner, A German surname that feels modern yet has historical depth, tying to trades like "chest maker."
