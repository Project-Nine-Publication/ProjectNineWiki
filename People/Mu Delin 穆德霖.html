Profile

Mu Delin (Chinese: 穆德霖; pinyin: Mù Délín) is a Chinese‑Canadian business executive best known as the founder and CEO of Delin Consultation Ltd., one of North America’s “Big Four” consulting firms. Under her leadership, Delin has become a global powerhouse in management, engineering, and technology advisory.

Early life and education

Born into a consulting family in Shanghai, Mu Delin is the elder daughter of Mu Zhixiong, who launched a small advisory practice in Osaka in 1992. She attended Shanghai International School (graduating top of her class in 1996) and went on to earn a Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering from the National Institute of Technology in 2000, graduating with the Institute’s President’s Award for Academic Excellence.

Career

Early consultancy (2000–2005)

Delin began her career at TechPro Consulting in Tokyo, working on process optimization and supply‑chain efficiency for automotive and electronics clients. By 2004, she was leading teams that achieved up to 20% reductions in production lead times.

Founding Delin Consultation Ltd. (2005)

In 2005, she moved to Toronto with seed funding from her family’s venture arm and launched Delin Consultation Ltd. As CEO, she secured early contracts with major retail chains and public‑sector transportation agencies, setting the stage for rapid growth.

Growth and recognition (2010–present)

  • 2010: Established Delin’s NFC Center of Excellence.
  • 2015: Delin ranked among North America’s “Big Four Consultants.”
  • 2018: Listed in Forbes’ “Top 50 Women in Consulting.”
  • 2021: Named to the Financial Times’ “Globe 100 People to Watch.”

She serves on the advisory boards of the International NFC Forum and the Global Smart Cities Alliance.

Other ventures

  • Automated Technologies Inc.: Co‑founder of the firm developing robotic 3D‑printing systems for construction.
  • Venture investments: Active investor in cleantech startups focusing on sustainable materials and green building solutions.

Personal life

Mu Delin resides in Toronto with her husband, Chen Wei, and their two children. Fluent in Mandarin, English, and Japanese, she is an amateur painter and certified yoga instructor. Her younger sister, Mu Meilin, serves as Delin’s CFO.
