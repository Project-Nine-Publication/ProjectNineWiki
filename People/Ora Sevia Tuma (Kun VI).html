Journey to Earth

The mission of Kun VI on the way to Earth is twofold: 1) Find out details regarding what happened and whereabouts of Kun VII; 2) Establish trade and military route to Earth with early investigation data and information. In fact, part of the construction work might have already been in place since Kun VII left or even earlier time - some routes can overlap well-established trading routes and depots between Doma and other systems.
