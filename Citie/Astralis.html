Country: Eldoria

History & Former Glory

  • Once a haven of wonder; astral arts integrated into daily life.
  • Golden fields, sapphire rivers, bustling markets.
  • Protected by wards powered by the sacred tree of Alhamra.

Present State:

  • Largely in ruins due to a prolonged siege.
  • Smoke and ash blanket the city; wards and spires are cracked/broken.
  • Constant bombardment; minimal supplies left (food, relics, khalin).

City

Key Structures:

  • Walls and Barricades once protected by runes but now crumbling.
  • Wards: Astral defenses that finally break at the chapter’s climax.
  • Underground Sanctum/Tunnels: A hidden refuge where the final decisions to flee are made.
