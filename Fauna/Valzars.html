Type: Animal Plural: Valzars Planet: Tellus

Archetype: Horse

This species could be somewhat horse-like in physiology—lean bodies, powerful legs, and sure-footed endurance over long distances—but the name “Valzars” immediately places them as a native creature unique to the Tellus world.

We might further define them with distinctive traits (can be magical), such as a ridge of stiff, iridescent bristles instead of a mane, or slightly elongated, claw-like hooves that help them grip rocky terrain.

Over time, “Valzars” becomes part of the setting’s common vocabulary, just as “horses” would be on Earth.

In Other Languages: English
