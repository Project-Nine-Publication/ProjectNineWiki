Does Ids has a "typical" Earth-like geology or something more "bizarre", e.g. like Mars?

Tags: #Unsolved

The question as to whether Ids even have plate tectonics is a big one, because Mars doesn't. The reality that without plate tectonics Mars can still have water and maybe even host life provides a very distinct view of the whole planet/continent concept compared to Earth (Tellus).
