Latter Day Praxism is a restorationist faith centered on the teachings of Praxis, a god who once walked among mortals but has since departed forever. In these final days, believers cherish newly revealed truths and a covenant that leads them toward moral growth, communal harmony, and spiritual fulfillment despite the deity’s permanent absence.
