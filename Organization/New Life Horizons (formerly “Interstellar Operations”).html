Overview

Full legal name: New Life Horizons Medical Research & Space Medicine Foundation
**Public tagline: “Advancing humanity one donor at a time.”

Public façade

A respected non-profit that funds zero-gravity medical trials and “extreme-environment longevity research.” They pay extremely well for healthy volunteers willing to undergo “indefinite off-world deployment for science.” Most recruits come from Relief Path Society’s Donated Exit stream, believing they are making the ultimate gift to medical progress.

Naming and Etymology

Interstellar Operation: 音斯太乐

Plot

Private reality
New Life Horizons is the polite, white-coat interface that buys the “rejects” from Méi Tù International—the ones not pretty enough for Medalian salons or stable enough for Krukian memory harvesting. They are used for:

  • Long-duration suspended-animation testing (often fatal)
  • Organ growth templates
  • Zero-g reproduction experiments run by rogue Ikurian geneticists
  • Spare parts for wealthy off-world clients who cannot wait for Méi Tù’s premium catalogue

The foundation’s headquarters is a gleaming research station in Tellus L4. Every cadaver is cremated, every data set classified, every relative sent a heartfelt letter and a generous compensation cheque. No bodies, no evidence, no crime.

Money flow (simplified)
Relief Path Society → sells “Final Gift” bodies to New Life Horizons for CAD $200 k–$1.2 M each
New Life Horizons → resells organs/data/templates to Ikurian and Krukian buyers for 5–20× markup
Méi Tù International → takes the top 3 % most beautiful for 50–200 M per head
Gao Cong → keeps 68 % of everything that touches Relief Path
Gao Shi Qiu (retired) → still receives a 4 % “consulting fee” on any deal that originated in old Kanit networks
