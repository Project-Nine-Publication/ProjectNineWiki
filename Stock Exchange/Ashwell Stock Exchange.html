Type: Stock Exchange Region: Commonwealth of Jorenia Short Name: ASE Location: Ashwell City, the capital and financial center of Jorenia.

History

Established in the early 1920s, the ASE played a key role in modernizing Jorenia’s economy. Over time, it facilitated capital growth for emerging industries—initially fisheries and agriculture, and later manufacturing, infrastructure, and technology sectors.

Regulation & Oversight

The ASE operates under the regulatory framework of the Jorenia Financial Services Commission (JFSC), ensuring transparency, investor protection, and compliance with national and international standards.

Indices

  • ASE Composite Index: Tracks the performance of the top 200 companies.
  • ASE GreenTech Index: Specially designed for Jorenia’s thriving renewable energy and environmental technology firms.
  • ASE Maritime & Resource Index: Focused on traditional strengths in fisheries, shipping, minerals, and forestry.
